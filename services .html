Mama Maendeleo Resort · Lamu County, Kenya

Our Services

Thoughtfully crafted to ensure every guest — whether travelling for business or leisure — enjoys genuine comfort, warmth, and the authentic spirit of the Swahili coast.

  • 🛏️

    Comfortable Accommodation

    Choose from 9 standard self-contained rooms, each thoughtfully furnished with a classic four-poster bed draped with a fresh mosquito net for your protection and style. Every room includes a dedicated working table and chair, a ceiling fan for cool tropical breezes, and complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi — striking the ideal balance between leisure relaxation and business productivity.

    9 Standard Rooms Available
  • 🍽️

    Authentic Swahili Dining

    Start your day with a hearty breakfast served fresh between 6:00 am and 10:00 am, and enjoy lunch and supper options throughout the day. Our kitchen draws deeply from the rich culinary traditions of Lamu — think aromatic coconut rice, freshly caught seafood, pilau spiced to perfection, and the warm, earthy flavours that define Swahili coastal cuisine. Every meal is a cultural journey on a plate.

    Breakfast · Lunch · Supper
  • 📶

    Free High-Speed Wi-Fi

    Stay connected throughout your entire stay with complimentary Wi-Fi available in all rooms and common areas. Whether you need to send reports, join virtual meetings, or simply share your Lamu adventure on social media, our reliable internet connection keeps you online without interruption — at no extra cost.

    Complimentary · All Rooms
  • 🔒

    24/7 Security & Safety

    Your safety is our top priority. The resort is enclosed within a high perimeter wall and monitored around the clock by a comprehensive CCTV surveillance system. Our proximity — just 0.5 km from Hindi Police Station — provides an added layer of assurance, so you can rest, explore, and conduct your business affairs with complete peace of mind.

    CCTV · Perimeter Wall · Police Proximity
  • 🧺

    Laundry Services

    Keep your wardrobe fresh during extended stays with our in-house laundry service. We handle your clothing with care, ensuring garments are cleaned, pressed, and returned promptly. Laundry charges are conveniently consolidated with your accommodation bill, making settlement simple and hassle-free at check-out or on a daily basis.

    On-Request · Billed with Accommodation
  • 🗺️

    Tourism & Cultural Excursions

    Perfectly positioned in Hindi town — less than 10 km from the beautiful Kitangani Beach — Mama Maendeleo Resort is your gateway to Lamu's UNESCO World Heritage attractions. Explore the ancient Lamu Old Town, sail on traditional dhows, discover mangrove forests, or witness the breathtaking Swahili architecture that has stood for centuries. Our team is happy to assist with trip arrangements and local insights.

    UNESCO Heritage · Beach · Cultural Tours
  • 💼

    Business & Corporate Services

    Catering equally to the business traveller, Mama Maendeleo Resort offers flexible corporate room rates and LPO-based billing for organisations and government agencies. With the expanding Lamu Port and LAPPSET corridor driving commercial activity in the region, our resort serves as a strategic base for professionals, contractors, and government delegations visiting the county. Corporate bookings are confirmed swiftly upon LPO submission.

    Corporate Rates · LPO Accepted · Conference Support
