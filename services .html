Our Services

Everything You Need
for a Remarkable Stay

From sun-up breakfast to secure evening rest, we ensure every moment at Mama Maendeleo is seamless, warm, and memorable.

Accommodation

Nine fully self-contained standard rooms with four-poster beds, fresh mosquito nets, working desks, ceiling fans, and free high-speed Wi-Fi — catering to both leisure and business guests.

Swahili Dining

Savour breakfast (6–10 am), lunch, and supper richly crafted from local Lamu cuisine traditions — coconut-infused dishes, fresh seafood, and aromatic Swahili spiced meals every day.

Free Wi-Fi

Complimentary high-speed wireless internet access throughout all rooms and common areas, keeping you connected for work, streaming, and sharing your Lamu adventures effortlessly.

24/7 Security

A robust high perimeter wall, full CCTV surveillance coverage, and proximity to Hindi Police Station (0.5 km away) ensure you and your belongings are safe at all hours.

Laundry Services

In-house laundry services available on request for extended stays. Charges are conveniently included in your daily or departure bill settlement, keeping things simple and stress-free.

Corporate Hosting

LPO-based billing, dedicated corporate rates, and flexible group arrangements make Mama Maendeleo the ideal base for delegations, contractors, and professionals working in the LAPPSET corridor.
